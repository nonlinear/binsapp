BinsApp Prototypes

Bins is divided in 4 main views:

Tag

or genres, is where readers browse titles and commit to it by following it.

Bin

is the title per se. covers are king here, advertising its content.

Mag

is the granular object, where user read. It uses flexslider, a gallery that works perfect both on web and touchscreens. Here, experience is broken. Check Dillon Mag (preferably on a tablet or phone, portrait mode) for a more complete experience.

User

is the bottom up discovery space, where you can find new titles per taste. We need to rethink it for the privacy-minded generation... Maybe profile shows only titles that reader actively liked, not just read. This becomes so their trophy shelf, displaying the titles they enjoyed most.
